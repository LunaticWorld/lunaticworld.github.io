# Privacy Policy

**[US Stock Disclosure Alerts]** (hereinafter referred to as the "Service") respects your privacy and does not collect unnecessary personal information. The Service can be used without any registration process, and the developer does not directly collect or store personal information such as your name, phone number, or email address.

## 1. Information Collection and Methods
The Service does not collect any personal information directly entered by the user. However, to provide core app functionalities and display advertisements, the following technical information may be automatically processed through third-party (Google) SDKs.

* **Advertising ID (ADID):** Used to display ads via Google AdMob.
* **Device Token (FCM Token):** Used to send real-time SEC disclosure push notifications.
* **Other:** App usage records, access logs, etc. (automatically generated for service stability).

## 2. Purpose of Use
The automatically processed technical information is used solely for the following purposes:

1. **Service Provision**
   - Sending US SEC (EDGAR) disclosure push notifications.
2. **Advertising**
   - Providing banner and interstitial ads within the app via Google AdMob.

## 3. Third-Party Data Provision
The Service does not share your personal information with third parties. However, we use the following external platform technologies to implement core app features:

| Third Party | Purpose of Provision | Data Provided |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Google LLC (Firebase)** | Push notification delivery | Device Token (FCM Token) |
| **Google LLC (AdMob)** | Personalized advertising | Advertising ID, Usage records |

## 4. User Rights and Choices
Users can stop information processing at any time by opting out of automatic collection via their device settings or by deleting the app.

* **Opting out of Ad ID collection:** Device Settings > Google > Ads > Delete advertising ID or Reset advertising ID
