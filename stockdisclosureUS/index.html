Stock Disclosure US Account Deletion

This page explains how users of Stock Disclosure US can delete their app account and request deletion of associated account data.

If you can still open the app, the fastest way to delete your account is inside the app.

Delete your account in the app

  1. Open Stock Disclosure US.
  2. Go to More.
  3. Open Account & credits.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Review the warning and confirm deletion.

If you cannot access the app

You can request account deletion by email: j12i29j12i29@gmail.com

Please include the following information so we can identify the account:

  • The Google account email address you used to sign in to Stock Disclosure US.
  • A short note saying that you want to delete your Stock Disclosure US account.

We may ask for additional confirmation if needed to verify account ownership. Please do not send passwords, payment details, or other sensitive information.

Data deleted

When your Stock Disclosure US account is deleted, the following account-related data is deleted:

  • Your app account linked through Supabase Auth / Google sign-in.
  • Your account credits.
  • Your invite code and invite-related account records.
  • Your saved account settings.

Data that may remain

  • Public SEC filing data is not deleted because it is public market disclosure information, not user account data.
  • Install-only guest usage data stored on your device may remain until you clear app data or uninstall the app.
  • Security, fraud-prevention, diagnostic, or legal-compliance logs may be retained for up to 90 days, unless a longer period is required by law.

Partial data deletion

Stock Disclosure US currently does not provide a separate self-service option to delete only part of your account data while keeping the account active. If you need help with a specific data request, contact us by email.

Processing time

In-app deletion is processed immediately when available. Email requests are reviewed and processed within a reasonable time, typically within 30 days after we verify the request.
